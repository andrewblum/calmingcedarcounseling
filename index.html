Mission

At Calming Cedar Counseling, we build strong therapeutic relationships through compassion, empathy, and unconditional positive regard while using strength-based interventions and psychoeducation to empower clients so they can be active agents of change in their own life story. The founder of Calming Cedar Counseling prioritizes the ACA code of ethics (2014) and evidence-based best practices to increase optimal outcomes for clients’ seeking treatment for a variety of concerns.

Who I Am

The founder of Calming Cedar Counseling PLLC, Allison O’Leary M.A., M.S., is passionate about helping others accept their unique truth in a world full of obstacles. She understands that change can be scary and strives to create a safe space for clients as they make meaningful changes toward optimal health and wellbeing. Allison believes in tailoring her therapeutic approach to fit the unique needs of each client or couple. Most importantly, she strives to create a warm and caring therapeutic environment for everyone. Allison has been in the mental health field for over a decade and has two masters in the field of psychology and mental health. She is a Licensed Associate Counselor (LAC) through the Arizona Board of Behavioral Health and a National Certified Counselor. Allison is currently in Supervised Private Practice and is supervised by Ellie Sivill, LPC, who has over 16 years of experience as a licensed clinical therapist.
